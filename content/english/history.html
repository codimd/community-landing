--- title: History ---

HedgeDoc is the community-driven fork of CodiMD, which is the open source counterpart to HackMD. Confused? Let us explain:

{{< history-entry graph="single point">}}

May '15

The first commit in the history of HackMD is made. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="single point">}}

July '15

The public instance hackmd.io goes online. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double split-start">}}

October '17

HackMD is split into HackMD CE and HackMD EE. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-left">}} The team behind HackMD continues the development in a private fork (HackMD EE) and sells it at hackmd.io. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-right">}} The community starts to work on the open source version (HackMD CE), but changed the license to AGPLv3. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-left">}} The HackMD EE team rewrites their private fork in TypeScript and sells it at hackmd.io. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-right">}}

June '18

The community renames HackMD CE to CodiMD to avoid name confusion. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-left">}}

June '18

The HackMD EE team proposes to replace the community-code of CodiMD with their rewrite of HackMD EE by switching to an open-core model. To use the core in their private fork, they want to re-license the new open source code under Apache 2.0. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="double point-right">}} The community wants to keep their code and the AGPLv3 license. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="void-left straight-right point-left">}} The HackMD team continues to develop HackMD EE. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="split-from-right double red">}} The community creates a hard fork of CodiMD into their own GitHub organisation {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left red void-right point-right">}} The HackMD team continues to maintain the CodiMD repo in the hackmd organisation. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}} The community maintains the hard fork of CodiMD repo. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}} People are confused about the difference between codimd/CodiMD and hackmd/CodiMD. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}}

April '20

The community begins the search for a new name. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}}

July '20

The community chooses "HedgeDoc" as the new name. Meanwhile they start a complete rewrite of CodiMD in TypeScript. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}}

September '20

Eric-G creates and submits a new logo for HedgeDoc. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="straight-left point-left red">}}

December '20

The community publishes HedgeDoc 1.7 with the new name and the new logo. {{< /history-entry >}} {{< history-entry graph="void-left point-left red">}}

2022

Tentative release date of HedgeDoc 2 {{< /history-entry >}}
